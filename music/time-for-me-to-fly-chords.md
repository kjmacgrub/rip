# Time for Me to Fly — REO Speedwagon

**Key:** D major
**Chords:** D, G, A
**Original tuning:** Open D (D-A-D-F#-A-D) — works fine in standard tuning

## Progression

```
Intro:  D  G  A  G  (repeat)

Verse:
D                          A
I've been around for you, been up and down for you
G              A              D
But I just can't get any relief

D                              A
I've swallowed my pride for you, lived and lied for you
G                 A            D
But you still make me feel like a thief

Chorus:
G                 A
It's time for me to fly
G                 A           D
Oh I've got to set myself free
G                 A
It's time for me to fly
G         A              D
That's just how it's got to be

Bridge:
G    A    D    (repeat)
```

## Notes

- Entire song is just D, G, A in various orders
- Verse: D - A - G - A - D
- Chorus: G - A - G - A - D
- Very adaptable for a cover arrangement
